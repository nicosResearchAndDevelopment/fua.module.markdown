## Symbols

- Copyright: © &copy; (c) (C)
- Registered trademark: ® &reg; (r) (R)
- Trademark: ™ &trade; (tm) (TM)
- Euro: € &euro;
- Left arrow: ← &larr;
- Up arrow: ↑ &uarr;
- Right arrow: → &rarr;
- Down arrow: ↓ &darr;
- Degree: ° &#176;
- Pi: π &#960;
- Plus-Minus: ± +-
- Dots: … ...
- Medium dash: &ndash; --
- Large dash: &mdash; ---
- Fractionals: ½ 1/2 ¼ 1/4 ¾ 3/4
- Multiplications: × x
